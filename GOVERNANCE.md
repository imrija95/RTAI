# Governance

RTAI uses a maintainer-led model. The copyright license grants broad rights to the code; it does
not transfer stewardship of the official project, release channels, roadmap, or name.

The lead maintainer sets the research direction, appoints additional maintainers, approves
releases, and makes final decisions when consensus is not reached. Contributors retain copyright
in their contributions and submit them under the repository license.

Sponsorship and donated compute support experiments but do not buy favorable results, roadmap
control, endorsements, or maintainer status. Experimental outcomes, including failures, remain
subject to the same evidence standards.

The project name and visual identity are not granted by the software license. Forks should avoid
presenting themselves as the official RTAI project.
